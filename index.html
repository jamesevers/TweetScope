Welcome to TwitterScope! TwitterScope is a visualization of tweets created across the country, giving us a glimpse of who's talking about what and where they're talking about it. I built the map with d3.js and captured the data using the Twitter API

Visualizing the tweets is easy:

  • Drag the slider to the right and the Tweets will appear on the map along with the hour of the day they were created and the total number of Tweets collected at that time
  • Hover over an individual tweet to see the content of the Tweet and the exact time it was created
  • The display below the slider shows the number of tweets, the time of day when they were collected, and popular hashtags
